Overview

This Financial and Inventory Management System allows users to efficiently manage and analyze their financial transactions and inventory levels. The system provides functionality for tracking expenses, generating various financial reports, and viewing real-time inventory data.

Creating and Managing Expenses

To create and manage expenses:

  1. Creating Expenses:
    • Navigate to the Create Expense page.
    • Fill in the details such as Date, Category, Amount, Description, and Supplier.
    • Click Save Expense to add the expense or Cancel to discard.
  2. Managing Expenses:
    • Navigate to the Expenses List page.
    • View, edit, or delete expenses from the list. Use the Details button for more information.

Inventory Management

To manage inventory and view reports:

  1. Managing Inventory:
    • Navigate to the Inventory page.
    • View, add, or remove items from the inventory.
  2. Inventory Report:
    • Navigate to the Inventory Report page.
    • View the bar chart displaying the current stock levels of inventory items.

Financial Reports

To access financial reports:

  1. General Financial Report:
    • Navigate to the Financial Reports page.
    • View the bar graph with data for inventory, sales, purchases, expenses, and profits.
  2. Daily Financial Report:
    • Navigate to the Daily Financial Report page.
    • View the line chart displaying daily financial summaries.

Financial Analysis

The financial analysis section helps you understand the performance of your business through various reports:

  • Inventory Analysis: Review the current stock levels and their impact on finances.
  • Expense Analysis: Analyze expense trends and their effects on overall financial health.
  • Sales and Purchases Analysis: Compare sales and purchase data to evaluate business performance.

Daily Reports

The daily reports provide insights into daily financial activities:

  • Accessing Daily Reports:
    • Navigate to the Daily Reports section.
    • Review daily summaries of sales, purchases, expenses, and profits.
